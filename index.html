Sports Day「スポーツの日」

Public Holiday in Japan

I think Sports Day should be designated and activated as a public holiday around the world.

When?

Every year on the Second Monday of October (JAPAN)
October 9 in 2023
October 14 in 2024

Sports Day must be a public holiday, and it must be on weekdays.
People can't enjoy sports because they are busy with work and study if it's in weekday and not be a public holiday.

Why?

Original Meaning from Cabinet Office

[JA]
スポーツを楽しみ、他者を尊重する精神を培うとともに、健康で活力ある社会の実現を願う。
[EN]
For providing an environment that pushes everyone from student to adult to pursue a healthy lifestyle.

What we think

Sports day can be a great way to show children how much fun taking part in sports can really be.
It also gives children the opportunity to showcase their talents to their families and friends.
As well as this, competing in sports days can

  • Improve health and fitness
  • Build leadership and teamwork skills
  • Improve social skills
  • Develop discipline
